# Privacy Policy — NewsSwipe
Last updated: February 22, 2026

## Overview
NewsSwipe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## Data We Collect
**Anonymous users:**
- Swipe interactions (left/right) stored anonymously with a session ID
- No name, email, or personal information collected
- No location data collected

**Registered users (optional):**
- Email address only
- Swipe interactions linked to your account

## How We Use Data
- To improve article recommendations
- To show trending content based on anonymous engagement data
- We never sell your data to third parties

## Third-Party Services
- Supabase (database & authentication): supabase.com/privacy
- NewsAPI (news content): newsapi.org/privacy

## Data Retention
- Anonymous session data: deleted after 72 hours
- Account data: retained until you delete your account
