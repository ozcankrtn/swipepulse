Privacy Policy — NewsSwipe

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Overview

NewsSwipe is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Data We Collect

Anonymous users:

  • Swipe interactions stored anonymously with a session ID
  • No name, email, or personal information collected
  • No location data collected

Registered users (optional):

  • Email address only
  • Swipe interactions linked to your account

How We Use Data

  • To improve article recommendations
  • To show trending content based on anonymous engagement
  • We never sell your data to third parties

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase (database & authentication): supabase.com/privacy
  • NewsAPI (news content): newsapi.org/privacy

Data Retention

Anonymous session data is deleted after 72 hours. Account data is retained until you delete your account.
